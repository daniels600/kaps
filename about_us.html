Our Mission

To educate our students to provide a high-quality, comprehensive, and meaningful education for all students

  • About Us
    Kwabenya Senior High School is a day community school located in Kwabenya, Ghana. It was commissioned on the 11th of November 2016 by the then President John Dramani Mahama.
  • Our Environment
    Our classrooms are spacious and very conducive for learning. Though we are fairly new, our students are very comfortable in our facilties. The external surroundings are a little noisy because we are near a busy road with commuters and passengers.
  • Sports Facilities
    There is a football field and basketball court for our students to train and also build themselves physically.
